The Vision & Strategic Blueprint

The Maker's Stay is founded on a sophisticated real estate and infrastructure investment thesis, not a traditional startup model. The project's phased approach, which first focuses on land acquisition and essential infrastructure, is a fiscally responsible model that de-risks the overall venture for subsequent phases of construction and operational launch.

A Phased Blueprint for Growth

Phase 1: Foundation

$3,400,000

Secure the foundational assets. This phase de-risks the entire venture by acquiring the land and building the core infrastructure.

  • → Land Acquisition (212 Acres)
  • → Existing House Retrofit
  • → Core Infrastructure (Roads, Energy, Water)

Phase 2: Construction

$1,782,350

Build the primary income-generating structures and key amenities, funded via a traditional construction loan collateralized by Phase 1 assets.

  • → Guest Accommodations
  • → Community Longhouse
  • → Grotto Pool & Greenhouse

Phase 3: Launch

$484,730

The final fit-out and operational ramp-up, funded through a combination of the construction loan and initial operational revenue.

  • → Furniture, Fixtures & Equipment
  • → Workshop & Kitchen Outfitting
  • → Working Capital for Ramp-Up
