A Living Model for a Regenerative Future

The Maker's Stay is a pioneering ecovillage in Michigan's Upper Peninsula, designed to be a sanctuary of creativity and a tangible model for regenerative development. Our core mission is to create a vibrant community that thrives in harmony with nature, demonstrating that sustainable living can be both beautiful and abundant.

Through our unique blend of hospitality, education, and sustainable housing, we are cultivating a new paradigm for conscious living. It's a dual-pronged approach that ensures both profitability and a lasting, positive impact on our world.

Amenities that Inspire

The Sunken Garden & Grotto Pool

The heart of our community is The Longhouse, which houses a magnificent greenhouse called The Sunken Garden. This space features a natural, chemical-free grotto pool with a waterfall, ensuring The Maker's Rest remains a compelling, all-season destination. The greenhouse also cultivates edible tropical plants, providing a unique on-site amenity and an educational experience.

This magnificent amenity, kept warm year-round, ensures the property remains a compelling destination throughout the off-season and serves as a living demonstration of regenerative food production.

Workshops & Creative Spaces

We believe in fostering an environment of creativity and learning. The Maker's Rest provides dedicated spaces for our guests to explore new skills and connect with their inner artist. All workshop participation requires a signed liability waiver to ensure a safe and secure experience for everyone.

Art & Craft Studio

A dedicated studio for painting, sketching, pottery, and other hands-on crafts. Our curated art kits provide all the materials needed to get started.

Wood & Metal Workshop

A fully equipped workshop for woodworking and metal crafting, where guests can learn to create custom pieces under the guidance of our expert instructors.

Culinary Kitchen

Our communal kitchen hosts farm-to-table culinary workshops, teaching guests how to prepare meals using fresh, on-site ingredients from our greenhouse and aquafarming system.

Rockhounding & Lapidary Arts

The Upper Peninsula is a rock collector's paradise, home to some of the most unique geological formations and minerals in the world. We offer guided rockhounding excursions for guests to explore our property and the surrounding area in search of Agates, Chlorastrolite (the state gem), and other treasures. Back at the ecovillage, our dedicated lapidary workshop allows guests to cut, shape, and polish their finds into beautiful, one-of-a-kind pieces of jewelry or art.

Guided Rockhounding Excursions

Explore the rugged landscape with our expert guides to find unique minerals and gems. All tools and safety equipment are provided.

Lapidary Workshop

Turn your discoveries into polished works of art. Our workshop is equipped with saws, grinders, and polishers for all skill levels. Liability waivers are required for all workshop participation.

From Dirt to Dazzle

The Non-Profit Heart of Our Community

From Dirt to Dazzle is the non-profit arm of The Maker's Trust. We believe in a regenerative ecovillage model that goes beyond sustainability to actively renew the environment. Our mission is to inspire American communities by demonstrating how stunning, sustainable homes and shared infrastructure can make a creative, nature-connected lifestyle both affordable and accessible for all.

Our Core Programs

Regenerative Education

We offer hands-on workshops on a wide range of regenerative practices, including:

  • Sustainable construction and Earthship design
  • Aquaculture and advanced food production systems
  • Permaculture, foraging, and biodynamic agriculture
  • Artistic and creative skills like painting, pottery, and metalworking

Community Advocacy & Outreach

We serve as a leader in advocating for regenerative development. Our work includes:

  • Securing grants to fund innovative, eco-friendly projects
  • Documenting and sharing our building practices with other communities
  • Fostering strong community bonds through shared purpose and a collaborative spirit
