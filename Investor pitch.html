Clear & Favorable Exit Strategies

Investor Profit Share

A direct equity model where investors receive a share of net profits, distributed annually. This provides a consistent, long-term return tied directly to the success of the ecovillage.

Community Buy-Out Option

Our ultimate goal. After a set period, the resident community cooperative will have the option to buy out the investors' stake at a fair market valuation, providing a significant lump-sum return and fulfilling the project's legacy.
