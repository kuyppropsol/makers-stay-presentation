The Maker's Stay

A Blueprint for a Regenerative Future

The Maker's Stay is more than a business venture; it is a visionary ecovillage conceived as a direct and strategic response to the systemic dynamics of modern economies. It is a pioneering enterprise, designed not only to be resilient in the face of economic uncertainty but to emerge from it as a vital hub for its surrounding community. This is achieved through a multi-layered framework of legal, financial, and operational redundancies that provide profound stability.

The Maker's Ethos

Our core identity is built on the narrative that a life of purpose, beauty, and creativity can be regenerative. We appeal to the "maker" in all of us—the dreamers, artists, farmers, and builders creating a life of purpose.

Immersive Experience

We offer a sanctuary of the senses, incorporating biophilic design to reduce stress and improve well-being. From "Wonderful Structures" earth homes to curated art kits for every guest, the experience is designed to be restorative and inspiring.

The Strategic Case for Michigan's Upper Peninsula

Our chosen 212-acre property in Michigan's Upper Peninsula is a strategic asset. The location offers a perfect convergence of natural resources, a thriving "maker" culture, and a supportive regulatory environment. This is not a choice of convenience, but a data-driven decision for long-term success.

Market Dominance

Michigan is a top performer in tourism, generating over $28 billion annually. The U.P. itself is a premier tourism destination with a 7.7% increase in visitor spending in 2023, showcasing a thriving regional market.

Cultural Alignment

The U.P. has an existing culture of craftsmanship and outdoor recreation. The creative sector in the region generated over $42.5 million in 2022, indicating a built-in audience for our "Maker's Ethos."

Supportive Environment

Michigan has specific laws supporting agritourism businesses, like the Farmland and Open Space Preservation Program, providing a clear path for development and long-term financial advantages.

A Phased Financial Blueprint for Success

Our project is structured into three distinct and sequential phases. This fiscally responsible approach de-risks the venture and ensures capital is deployed strategically and efficiently. The total project cost is estimated at $5,667,080.

Phase 1: Foundational Investment

$3,400,000

Securing the 212-acre property and building the essential, non-negotiable infrastructure. This makes the property shovel-ready for all future development.

Phase 2: Construction & Amenities

$1,782,350

Focuses exclusively on the construction of the primary structures and initial amenities, including guest accommodations and the grotto pool.

Phase 3: Operational Launch

$484,730

Covers the costs for getting the business fully operational, including all furnishings, workshop equipment, and working capital for a successful launch.
