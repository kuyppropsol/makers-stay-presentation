Our Vision

Welcome. We are pioneering a new standard for American home life by creating a community that is both financially viable and deeply regenerative. The Maker's Stay is a revolutionary project designed to meet the growing demand for authentic, sustainable, and purpose-driven living. We are building more than a business; we are building a blueprint for a life you'll never need to escape from.

The Opportunity

The market is ready for this. We are tapping into three high-growth sectors, and The Maker's Stay is the perfect convergence of these trends, providing a diversified, resilient, and high-margin revenue model.

The numbers don't lie. Ecotourism, agritourism, and the creator economy are all experiencing explosive growth. Our integrated model allows us to capture value from all three, making our business inherently resilient to market fluctuations.

Our Business Model

The Maker's Stay is a for-profit ecovillage operated by **Kuyper Property Solutions**. We are creating a three-pillar business designed for success.

Regenerative Retreats

Our luxury eco-accommodations, including our "Royal" and "Terranova" models, will offer guests a unique and restorative experience. Revenue is generated through nightly rentals, with a focus on delivering a premium experience that justifies high rates. These units are designed with a deep connection to the natural landscape, enhancing the guest experience and promoting repeat business.

Farm-to-Table Experience

Our on-site regenerative farm is the heart of our community. Utilizing advanced agricultural practices, it will supply our farm-to-table eatery, creating a zero-mile food system. The farm also generates revenue through a direct-to-consumer farm stand, providing a consistent income stream from both guests and the local community.

Maker Workshops

This segment is the soul of our experiential model. We will host expert-led workshops in traditional crafts like pottery and blacksmithing, as well as sustainable living skills. These high-margin events not only generate revenue but also foster a vibrant, engaged community and attract a dedicated audience throughout all seasons.

Our Strategic Advantage

Our vision is supported by a deep understanding of market trends, a strong team, and a commitment to a new way of living. We have built-in advantages that ensure success.

Why Michigan?

Our site selection was a data-driven process that analyzed natural resources, regulatory environment, and demographics. The **215-acre property in Ontonagon** with Lake Superior frontage provides the perfect foundation for our vision.

Our Team

We have a collective of professionals with extensive, hands-on experience in construction, welding, and logistics. This in-house expertise allows us to execute our physical development efficiently and cost-effectively, reducing reliance on expensive contractors.

Silent Accessibility

Our commitment to **universal design** creates environments that are barrier-free and universally welcoming. This philosophy attracts a wider demographic and builds a community where everyone feels a sense of true belonging, providing a key competitive advantage.

The Community Land Trust (CLT)

This is the visionary core of our model. The land will be placed in a **Community Land Trust (CLT)**, a legal structure that separates land ownership from the ownership of homes. This ensures the ecovillage remains affordable and protected from market speculation, securing its long-term purpose.

This structure empowers our **co-creators** to actively participate in the community's governance, fostering a deep sense of ownership and well-being. It is a replicable and scalable solution for building resilient, self-governing communities.
