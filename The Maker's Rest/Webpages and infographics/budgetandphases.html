Building & Infrastructure Phasing

The physical development is structured in three distinct phases, independent of business operations, to ensure methodical and sustainable growth.

Phase 1: Foundational Development (Total: $3,524,220)

This phase establishes all primary infrastructure, the main communal building (The Longhouse), and key operational assets to begin generating revenue immediately.

  • ■ Land Acquisition ($1,900,000)
  • ■ Longhouse & Infrastructure ($1,424,220)
  • ■ Existing House Retrofit ($350,000)

This phase includes the core, long-term physical assets that form the foundation of The Maker's Stay.

Phase 2: Expanded Community (Total: ~ $1.5M - $2M est.)

This phase focuses on building additional residential and communal structures to scale capacity and attract long-term residents. The final budget for this phase will be refined during Phase 1 operations.

Phase 3: Public Engagement & Growth (Total: ~ $5M+ est.)

The final phase expands the project into a full-scale public-facing ecovillage, adding large-scale public amenities and a commercial hub. The budget is speculative and will be refined over time.
