Executive Summary

The Maker's Stay is a visionary ecovillage conceived as a direct and strategic response to the systemic vulnerabilities of modern economies. Our pioneering enterprise is designed to be a resilient, self-sufficient community hub that provides profound social utility and a viable post-crisis economic paradigm. We are seeking an investment of **$3,400,000** for Phase 1 to acquire 212 acres and build foundational infrastructure.

Exit Strategy

Our exit strategy is designed to align with our mission of long-term community resilience. The primary pathway for investor returns is consistent, long-term **profit sharing through regular dividends**. A "Community Buy-Out" option also provides a clean exit for investors, allowing the resident community to buy out the initial investors' ownership stake at a fair market valuation after a predetermined period.
