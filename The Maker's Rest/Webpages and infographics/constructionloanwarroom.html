Anticipated Questions & Answers

Q: What if your occupancy rates are too low?

A: "Our model is diversified, so we don't rely on just one revenue stream. I can show you in our interactive model how we remain profitable even with conservative assumptions."

Q: Why a Community Land Trust? Doesn't that make things complicated?

A: "On the contrary, the CLT simplifies our long-term vision. It's the only way to protect the land from speculation and ensure the project's core mission is fulfilled. It's a strategic decision that guarantees the project's future."

Q: How does From Dirt to Dazzle tie in?

A: "From Dirt to Dazzle is a separate, non-profit partner. It strengthens our for-profit business by building goodwill, community engagement, and brand loyalty. The two are symbiotic."

Q: What's the value of your team's experience?

A: "Our team's hands-on skills in construction, welding, and logistics directly translate to significant cost savings. We can do much of the physical work ourselves, which reduces our budget and project risk for you."
