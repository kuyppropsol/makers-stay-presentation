A Phased Financial Blueprint for Success

Our project is structured into three distinct and sequential phases, representing a total estimated cost of $5,667,080. This fiscally responsible approach is designed to de-risk the venture by achieving critical milestones before proceeding to subsequent stages. Capital is deployed strategically and efficiently, with each phase building upon the tangible assets and successes of the last.

Phase 1: Foundational Investment - $3,400,000

This initial phase is a focused capital raise from investors. The primary objective is to secure the land and build the essential, non-negotiable infrastructure required for the entire project. This includes:

  • Land Acquisition (212 acres): $1,900,000
    • Includes negotiated purchase price, closing costs, due diligence, legal fees, and initial survey work.
  • Existing House Retrofit: $350,000
    • Architectural & Design Fees: $25,000
    • Structural & Systems Upgrades: $125,000 (Foundation, framing, roof, electrical, plumbing, HVAC).
    • Interior Finishing: $150,000 (Drywall, flooring, custom cabinetry, fixtures, windows, doors).
    • Exterior Improvements: $50,000 (Siding, roofing repairs, paint, landscaping).
  • Core Infrastructure (Roads, Energy, Water/Septic): $1,150,000
    • Roads & Pathways: $350,000
    • Decentralized Renewable Energy System: $450,000
    • Water & Septic Systems: $350,000

Upon completion, Phase 1 renders the property "shovel-ready" for all future development. This significantly de-risks the project and creates a valuable, tangible asset base to secure favorable financing for Phase 2.

Phase 2: Construction & Amenities - $1,782,350

Funding for this phase will be secured via a traditional construction loan, using the newly acquired and developed land from Phase 1 as collateral. This phase focuses exclusively on constructing the physical structures and initial amenities that will generate revenue and define the guest experience:

  • Primary Structures (Wonderful Structures, Longhouse): $1,002,350
    • Wonderful Structures & Pads: $578,130 (Includes 4 Royal Cabins, 2 one-bedroom Terranovas, 2 two-bedroom Terranovas, plus pads and site prep).
    • Longhouse Construction: $424,220
  • Initial Amenities (Fleet Vehicles, Grotto Pool, Dog Park): $780,000
    • Fleet Vehicles & Equipment: $400,000 (2 AWD vans, heavy-duty tractor, UTV, snowmobile & ATV).
    • Grotto Pool & Greenhouse Structure: $330,000 (Engineering, greenhouse structure, natural pool, heated flooring, landscaping).
    • Indoor/Outdoor Dog Park: $50,000

Phase 3: Operational Launch - $484,730

The final phase covers all costs required to get the business fully operational. This funding is projected to come from a combination of the remaining construction loan and initial operational revenue. It includes:

  • Initial Furnishings, Fixtures & Decor: $250,000
  • Workshop & Kitchen Equipment: $59,730
    • Includes comprehensive outfitting for studios in woodworking, metal arts, jewelry, pottery, music, digital arts, performing arts, and painting.
  • Working Capital (3-6 months of operating expenses): $175,000
    • Covers initial payroll, marketing, and unforeseen costs before revenue streams are fully established.
