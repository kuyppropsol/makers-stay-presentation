The Maker's Stay: A Vision for Creativity

A Phased Financial Blueprint for Our Artisan Workshops

Total Initial Workshop Investment

$350,000

This represents the comprehensive budget to fully equip eight distinct, state-of-the-art workshop spaces, creating the heart of our hands-on, experiential community.

Project Funding Overview

The workshop budget is a strategic component of the total Phase 1 funding request of $4,054,220. While significant, it is a targeted investment in the core revenue-generating experiences that define The Maker's Stay.

Phased Workshop Rollout

Our development is structured in three strategic phases to manage capital expenditure and build operational momentum.

Phase 1: Foundational Makerspaces (Total: $145,000)

This initial phase establishes the core, high-demand workshops that will attract a broad range of makers and form the foundation of our community programming.

Woodworking ($65,000)

Pottery & Ceramics ($45,000)

Digital Arts ($35,000)

Phase 2: Specialized Craft & Artistry (Total: $120,000)

Building on our foundation, Phase 2 introduces workshops for specialized, high-value crafts, requiring dedicated equipment and controlled environments.

Metal Art & Forging ($75,000)

Lapidary & Jewelry Making ($45,000)

Phase 3: Expressive & Performing Arts (Total: $85,000)

The final phase expands our creative ecosystem to include spaces for fine arts, music, and performance, completing our multidisciplinary vision.

Painting & Fine Arts ($25,000)

Music Studio ($40,000)

Performing Arts ($20,000)
